Fantasy Football Players 22/23

Players to watch in the upcoming session

  1. Erling Haaland - Enter the goal machine. After only 4 matches, Haaland leads the way in the race for the golden boot. A high scoring Man City with an injury free Haaland is to be feared.
  2. Kevin De Bruyne - A semi-fit KDB finished in last year's top 10 points scorers. The possibilities are endless for a fully fit and firing KDB. The one reservation and consideration is whether Man City will continue with a false nine now that they have one of the world's best no. 9s in Haaland. This may lower KDBs scoring potential. Even with that, he still makes the list.
  3. Harry Kane - The striker that doesn't score in August or does he? Off to a bright start having already 4 in 4 only bodes well in year that Harry will want to be in form going into the World Cup.
  4. Alexander Mitrovic - The surprise inclusion but after breaking all previous Championship records last season, I expect good things from Mitrovic this season. Mitrovic is likely to deliver well on points per mil ratio.
  5. Mo Salah - Last season's highest scoring player is impossible to look past. Not only a consistent points scorer but an incredibly reliable captain. A MUST-HAVE player!
